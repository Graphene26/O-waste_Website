It's time to Manage Your Waste Responsibly

Our mission is to provide AI-powered, self-sustaining waste sorting solutions. In 2024, we implemented our technology at the Hejo Salamina waste bank in Bandung, Indonesia, and helped over 1,000 households manage their waste more efficiently. O’waste is also supported by the "Zero Waste" community, now with over 200 passionate members.
